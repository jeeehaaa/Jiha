Timeline of Tasks in the Internship

Medical Record Unit

This department is in charge of managing digital medical records and patient files. They control the setup and upkeep of electronic medical record systems and ensure that physical records are correctly arranged, stored, and accessible.

Week 1 - Week 2

Medical Report Unit

This unit is in charge of administering medical reports prepared by healthcare professionals. They make certain that medical reports are delivered on time and accurately to all relevant individuals, such as patients and healthcare professionals.

Week 3 - Week 4

Casemix and Statistics

This unit is working on the casemix management, including classification and grouping of patients according to their diagnoses. They also deal with health informatics, which is collecting and analyzing medical data for research purposes, quality improvement tasks, and decision-making.
