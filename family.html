Me and My Sisters

About Me and My Sisters

I have two younger sisters and we are all girls, so we always have girl's nights. We spend time together playing games or teaching our little sister how to write. Our presence is very important to our youngest sister because there is a big age gap between us, and she doesn't have friends her age to play with.

Although there are always many fights between us, starting from pulling each other's hair to stealing each other's things, we are always there for each other. I am grateful to have them in my life because they are the ones who always brighten the atmosphere at home.
